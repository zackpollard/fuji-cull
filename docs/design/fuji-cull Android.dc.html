WHY — DECISIONS ON THE THUMBS

The keyboard’s W/S becomes a three-up REJECT / CLEAR / KEEP bar pinned to the bottom, each a 56dp target within thumb reach. Tap a tile to open the paged viewer; swipe left/right pages shots.

WHY — DECODER HONESTY

A 4:2:2 clip that hardware can’t decode may freeze for a beat; the chip says “software decoder” so a stutter reads as a known fallback, not a bug. Same 4 GiB preview wall as everywhere.

WHY — DRAG SCRUBBER

The right-edge scrubber fades in on scroll and shows a month bubble under the thumb — the only fast way to cross 25k shots on a phone. Labels thin as the card grows.
